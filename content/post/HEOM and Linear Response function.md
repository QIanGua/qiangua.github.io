**Keywords**

**Hamiltonian:**

SBM + Bath(Ohimc/Subohmic) + Force
$$
H_{tot}(t) = \hat{H}_0  - F(t)\hat{B}_1,
$$


Eg:
$$
F(t) = \lambda e^{-t},
$$


**Response function:**
$$
R^{(1)}(t_1) = \frac{i}{\hbar}\langle[\hat{A}(t_1),\hat{B}_1]\rangle_{eq},
$$
